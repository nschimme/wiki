Certain aspects of the game depend on your **hometown**, such as
calculating [rent](./Rent.md) costs. Rent costs are lower in
hometowns than other towns. Note! You must be a
[citizen](./Citizen.md) to get this bonus.

These are the races town officials recognize as being at home:

- [Anduin Vale](./Anduin_Vale.md): Beornings
- [Blue Mountains](./Blue_Mountains.md): Dwarves
- [Fornost](./Fornost.md): Half-elves, Men
- [Grey Havens](./Grey_Havens.md): Elves
- [Lothlórien](./Lothlórien.md): Half-elves, Elves
- [Rivendell](./Rivendell.md): Half-elves, Elves
- [Shire](./Shire.md): Hobbits
- [Tharbad](./Tharbad.md): Men

Darkie officials have no racial prejudices (apart from hatred of
others).
