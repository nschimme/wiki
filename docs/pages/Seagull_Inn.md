The **Seagull Inn** is an [inn](./Inn.md) in
Harlond. [Eldinor the
bartender](./Eldinor_the_bartender.md) sells drinks and snacks on
the bottom floor, while an elven innkeeper
[rents](./Rent.md) out rooms on the top floor.

## Menu

- [pastries](./Pastry.md)
- [beer bottles](./Beer_bottle.md) full of beer
- [ceramic cups](./Ceramic_cup.md) full of tea
- small bottles full of
  whisky

The bar is open from 10am to 2pm and 5pm to 12am.
