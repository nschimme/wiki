The **Seagull Inn** is an [inn](./inn.md) in
[Harlond](./Harlond.md). [Eldinor the
bartender](./Eldinor_the_bartender.md) sells drinks and snacks on
the bottom floor, while an [elven innkeeper](./elven_innkeeper.md)
[rents](./rent.md) out rooms on the top floor.

## Menu

- [pastries](./pastry.md)
- [beer bottles](./beer_bottle.md) full of [beer](./beer.md)
- [ceramic cups](./ceramic_cup.md) full of [tea](./tea.md)
- [small bottles](./small_bottle.md) full of
  [whisky](./whisky.md)

The bar is open from 10am to 2pm and 5pm to 12am.
