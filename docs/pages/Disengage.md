**Disengage** is a [delayed](./Delayed.md)
[command](./commands.md) that allows you to stop the
[fight](./Combat.md) unless you are the character who is fighting
the opponent directly and taking his strokes (the
"tank/[buffer](./buffer.md)"). This takes a little while.

See also: [Escape](./Escape.md), [Flee](./Flee.md),
[Wimpy](./Wimpy.md)
