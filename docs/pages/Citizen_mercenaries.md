title: Citizen mercenaries
description: 'Citizen mercenaries are available for hire in Bree'
---

Citizen mercenaries are available for hire in [Bree](./Bree.md)
and [Fornost](./Fornost.md). Costs 10 silver pieces each per day.
They will follow you and assist you in combat. They help with [parry
splitting](/pages/Combat).
