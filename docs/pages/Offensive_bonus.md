The **offensive bonus** (OB%) increases the likelihood of your attacks
hitting a target. OB is heavily dependent on the
[weapon](./Weapon.md) you're using and your
[skill](./Skill.md) with that weapon, as well as your
[mood](./Mood.md). There are also various pieces of equipment that
provide bonuses to OB.
