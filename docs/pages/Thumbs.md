The **thumbs** command is like a normal [social](./Socials.md)
with a minor twist. You can either thumbs up &lt;someone&gt; or thumbs down
&lt;someone&gt; which will make a positive or negative sign, respectively, to
someone.

See also: [Socials](./Socials.md)
