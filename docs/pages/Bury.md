Syntax: **bury** corpse

Allows you to build a mound and to bury a [corpse](./corpse.md)
inside it. The mound will slowly disappear over time, but it is rumoured
that [searching](./search.md) the place can yield some remains of
the possessions of the buried corpse. Mounds can be
[opened](./open.md) to reveal their contents.

See also: [Drag](./Drag.md), [Burn](./Burn.md),
[Decapitate](./Decapitate.md), [Drain](./Drain.md),
[Hang](./Hang.md)
