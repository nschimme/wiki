A list of **cloaks**. Cloaks are worn around the body for protection and
stealth. Animal furs can also be worn as a cloak. Cloaks help mostly
with [dodging](./Dodge_bonus.md), while furs worn as a cloak help
with [attacking](./Offensive_bonus.md). Some furs will keep you
warm, reducing the risk of a [flu](./Disease.md).
