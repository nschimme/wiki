**Scrolls** are powerful items which hold enchantments that can be read
by any character (even [trolls](./troll.md)!). Because they are
very powerful, some can be rather difficult to get.
[Orcs](./Orc.md) receive an [azure
scroll](./azure_scroll.md) from sage for each whitie
[focus](./focus.md) they hand in, and can also receive one for
each pbs. If the person reciting the scroll has also learned the
[spell](./spell.md) that the scroll contains, the reciting speed
will be greatly increased.
