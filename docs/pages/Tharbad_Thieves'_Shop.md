A **grocer** tends to the **Thieves' Shop** in
[Tharbad](./Tharbad.md). The store is open from 7am to 12pm and
2pm to 7pm, and stocks jewellery, [lockpicks](./lockpicks.md),
[pipes](./pipe.md) and [pipe-weed](./pipe-weed.md), some
[keys](./key.md), [lanterns](./lantern.md) and
[moneybags](./moneybag.md).
