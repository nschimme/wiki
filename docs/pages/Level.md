With every new level, [practice](./Practice.md) points become
available, that can be used in [guilds](./Guild.md) to learn new
skills.

The command [Level](./Level.md) lists the experience and travel
points required for your current level and for a few higher levels.

Example: Level 1 1 exp, 0 tp Level 2 1000 exp, 100 tp Level 3 3000 exp,
300 tp Level 4 7000 exp, 600 tp Level 5 14500 exp, 1000 tp

See also: XP, TP,
[Practice](./Practice.md), [Guild](./Guild.md)
