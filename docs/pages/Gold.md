*Copper redirects here. For the magical ring made from the metal, see
[copper ring](./Copper_ring.md).*

One **gold** coin = twenty **silver** pennies = two thousand **copper**
pennies.

Elves have elven names for the same money. Use them instead if you are
an [Elf](./Elf.md). (lauren = gold, celeb = silver, busc = copper)

To [drop](./Drop.md), or [give](./Give.md) money, use
&lt;amount&gt; &lt;kind&gt;, as in:

` > give 4 silver bilbo`

When referring to an unknown amount, use 'coins':

` > take coins`

Money is useful for buying things, and for [storing
your equipment](./Rent.md) while you are not playing.
