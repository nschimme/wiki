The **mage** is a class of powerful and violent magic, however it does
not possess the same healing powers as a [cleric](./cleric.md).
Protective [spells](./spell.md) exist, which are quite effective
at absorbing and dodging enemy damage.

### Opinion

Mages are master damage dealers who can [teleport](./teleport.md),
[charm animals](./charm.md), and cast [area of effect
spells](./earthquake.md). Being a caster, they are more difficult
to play than other classes.
