The continent of [Middle-earth](./Location) was shaped in the most ancient days of
Arda. It lay to the east of the other great continent of
[Aman](./Aman.md) (Undying Lands), and was separated from
[Middle-earth](./Location) by the Great Sea Belegaer.

At the end of the Third Age, the continent of Aman finally departs from
Arda, leaving the mortal realm of [Middle-earth](./Location) which would eventually
evolve into Europe, Asia, Africa and Australia.
