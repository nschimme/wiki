**Kick** is a [warrior](./Warrior.md) [skill](./Skill.md) and delayed command.

Kicking can be quite useful for the lonely warrior fighting foul spectres. When you kick someone you might cause it to lose its balance, giving you time for a fast hit. Timing is crucial, however. A skilful warrior will kick the opponent just before he hits, and follow it by a shattering blow.

## Mechanics

- **Prerequisites:** To master this skill, great [agility](../pages/MUME_IV_as_described_in_Estonia.md#scoring-and-attributes), lots of [muscle](../pages/MUME_IV_as_described_in_Estonia.md#scoring-and-attributes) and good eyesight are required.
- **Success:** The success of a kick depends upon how well you learned the skill. The better you are at kicking, the harder you kick.

## Example

`> kick goblin`
