A list of items worn around the **neck**. Those which are useful are
almost always magical, with the non magical items being of a purely
cosmetic or roleplaying nature. Usually magical items worn around the
neck provide the user with some protection against magic.

## Magical

- bright red amulet
- black amulet
- [necklace](./Necklace.md)
- old length of iron chain
- [small crystal phial](./Small_crystal_phial.md)
- pale blue stone

## Ordinary

- [blackened pendant](./Blackened_pendant.md)
- spiked collar
- [studded leather collar](./Studded_leather_collar.md)
- chequered scarf
- [plain scarf](./Plain_scarf.md)
- [red ruby](./Red_ruby.md)
- [sun-emblazoned cloakpin](./Sun-emblazoned_cloakpin.md)
