Command **FAQ** allows you to read the FAQ [board](./board.md)
where the most frequently asked questions are answered. For the syntax
of command 'FAQ', see '[news](./news.md)'.

The following FAQ files for new players are also available:

- [FAQ1](./FAQ1.md) - Frequently Asked Questions, part 1: generic
  help
- [FAQ2](./FAQ2.md) - Frequently Asked Questions, part 2:
  [renting](./rent.md) at an inn
- [FAQ3](./FAQ3.md) - Frequently Asked Questions, part 3: using
  [equipment](./equipment.md)
- [FAQ4](./FAQ4.md) - Frequently Asked Questions, part 4: hunting
  and [fishing](./fish.md)
- [FAQ5](./FAQ5.md) - Frequently Asked Questions, part 5:
  [mail](./mail.md), [boards](./board.md), books
