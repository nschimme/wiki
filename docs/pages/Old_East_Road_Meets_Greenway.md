This is an intersection of roads to the west of [Bree](./Bree.md).
The [Old East Road](./Old_East_Road.md) going from
[Bree](./Bree.md) to the [Grey Havens](./Grey_Havens.md)
crosses the [Greenway](./Greenway.md) going from
[Tharbad](./Tharbad.md) to [Fornost](./Fornost.md) here.
