*This article is about a type of mobile. For other plants, see
[herb](./Herb.md).*

**Plants** are a type of [mobile](./Mobile.md) that includes
[Ents](./Ent.md) and other animated trees, vines, fungi etc.
Plants are vulnerable to fire, so [spells](./Spell.md) like
[Burning Hands](./Burning_Hands.md) or
[Fireball](./Fireball.md) deal extra damage to them. Most plants
have no eyes and thus are not affected by the
[Blindness](./Blindness.md) spell.
