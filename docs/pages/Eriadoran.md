The **Eriadoran** people are the common people of Northwest [Middle-earth](./Location). Their distant ancestors are the
[Dunlendings](./Dunlendings.md) who settled Eriador in the First
Age. A portion of their people migrated across the Ered Luin and are
counted among the Edain. In Eriador they settled countless villages, but
their most well known establishment is [Bree](./Bree.md).

## Eriadoran Men in Mume

Eriadoran [Men](./Men.md) have the following modifications:

- They start with 5 additional practice points
- They can obtain Tharbad citizenship more easily

Eriadoran men can start the game in the [Tower
Hills](./Tower_Hills.md), [Fornost](./Fornost.md), or
[Bree](./Bree.md).
