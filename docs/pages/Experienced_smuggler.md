Experienced **smugglers** are aggressive, [evil](./alignment.md)
[level](./level.md) ~11-12 [mobiles](./mobile.md) found in
various places across [Arda](./Arda.md), especially common in the
areas near [Tharbad](./Tharbad.md). They load 15
[silver](./gold.md), a sword and some cloth equipment.

`An experienced smuggler is here, conspiring with his cohorts.`
`>`**`examine smuggler`**
`This is probably one of the many criminals that creep out of Tharbad.  He is`
`dressed in black, from head to toe.  He moves with fluid stealth and dexterity.`
`He attacks you fast as lightning.`
