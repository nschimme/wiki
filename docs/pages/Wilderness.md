**Wilderness** is a [ranger](./General.md) [skill](./Skill.md) that allows one to survive better in the wild.

## Effects

- **Movement:** You will be able to move swifter, easier, and more silently through dense forests, misty marshes, steep mountain passes, and frozen plains.
- **Nourishment:** Gives you the ability to get nourishment from plants and roots, reducing the need for food and water.
- **Crafts:** Assists in skills such as building campfires and fishing.
- **Tracking:** When combined with [awareness](./Awareness.md), it assists in picking out the least tiring routes when following [tracks](./Track.md).

## Mastering

Learning wilderness helps reduce the amount of work required to travel. The ease of learning the skill depends on [strength](../pages/MUME_IV_as_described_in_Estonia.md#scoring-and-attributes) and [willpower](../pages/MUME_IV_as_described_in_Estonia.md#scoring-and-attributes).

## Affinity

[Silvan](./Elf.md) Elves have a natural affinity for surviving in the wilderness, though this does not affect the speed at which they practice the skill.
