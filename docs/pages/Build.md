These commands are used by M level maiar or lower to manipulate the
**building** of new god [rooms](./room.md) or [legend
homes](./Legend_Homes.md).

'build info' will show the status of the room.

'build room &lt;args&gt;' will set the room informations.

Major help is available under the object 'Mortal's Guide to Building'
that will be given by [Ainur](./Ainur.md) when the rooms are
attributed.
