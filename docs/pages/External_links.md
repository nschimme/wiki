This page lists **links** to sites that concern MUME directly, as well as sites that contribute to the works of JRR [Tolkien](./Tolkien.md).

## MUME Official & Community

- [**MUME Official Site**](https://mume.org/) — News, character management, and official rules.
- [**MUME Forum**](https://mume.org/forum) — Community discussion and announcements.
- [**MUME Discord**](https://discord.gg/mume) — Real-time chat with the player base.
- [**Elvenrunes**](https://www.elvenrunes.com) — Logs, discussions, and character profiles.
- [**MUME Log Search**](https://logs.mume.org/) — Search through years of game logs and history.
- [Reddit](https://old.reddit.com/r/mume/) — MUME subreddit.
- [Facebook](https://www.facebook.com/groups/mumemud/) — MUME on Facebook.

## Mappers & Clients

- [**MMapper**](https://github.com/MUME/MMapper) — The official and most popular mapping tool. Essential for navigation.
- [**Mudlet**](https://www.mudlet.org/) — A modern, cross-platform MUD client.
- [**Mudlet MUME Scripts**](https://sites.google.com/view/mume-mudlet) — Essential scripts for playing MUME on Mudlet.
- [Nick's setup](https://github.com/MUME/mushclient-mume) — For visually impaired players.

## Tools & Information

- [**Character Planner**](https://mume.org/planner) — Plan your practices and stats before committing in-game.
- [**Jahara’s Stat Generator**](https://mume.schimmelmann.us/stats.htm) — Understanding how base stats affect spells and skills.
- [**mumewiki.kalka.org**](https://mumewiki.kalka.org/start) — Wojtek's inofficial MUME wiki with advanced info.
- [**Faine’s Page**](http://faine.epizy.com/) — Advanced info and guides.
- [**Krydos' Item Info**](https://krydos.com/mume/) — Detailed item information.
- [**How to Roar**](http://mume.digitar.ee/) — Website for Bears and Beornings with tips and hits of how to play.
- [Radagast/Slayah's Youtube](https://www.youtube.com/@Radagastthe1st) — MUME podcasts and interviews.
- [Nazgum's Video Logs](https://nazgum.com/mume/) — Video logs from MUME.
