# Endurance

**Endurance** is a [warrior](./Warrior.md) [skill](./Skill.md) that hardens your body, making you more resilient in combat and travel.

## Effects

- **Hardiness:** Allows you to suffer more damage in combat.
- **Vigour:** You can travel farther before becoming exhausted.
- **Healing:** Your body is rejuvenated, meaning scars heal faster and wounds wane.
- **Second Wind:** The most adept practitioners can even gain a "second wind" of vigour.

## Affinity

[Stoor](./Hobbit.md#stoor) Hobbits have a natural affinity for endurance due to their stoutness. This bonus is innate and does not make practicing the skill less effective.
