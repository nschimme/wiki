*For larger ships, see [ferry](./ferry.md).*

**Boats** are a special type of equipment that can be used to traverse
[waterways](./waterways.md) without needing to
[swim](./swim.md). To use a boat, you simply need to have it in
your [inventory](./inventory.md) when entering a body of water.
[Mounts](./Mount.md) can also follow you into water when given a
boat.

## Boats

- [brightly painted skiff](./brightly_painted_skiff.md)
- [canoe](./canoe.md)
- [light boat of reeds](./light_boat_of_reeds.md)
- [narrow grey boat](./narrow_grey_boat.md)
- [raft](./raft.md)
