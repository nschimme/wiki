# Leadership

**Leadership** is a [ranger](./General.md)
[skill](./Skill.md) that allows one to guide a group through rough
[terrain](./Terrain.md) more efficiently.

## Effects

- **Efficiency:** A skilled leader moves on hidden animal paths to maintain speed and safeguard the group's strength.
- **Stealth:** Adds discipline to the group, reducing the noise made.
- **Riding:** Reduces the time taken to ride and lead mounts.
- **Prerequisites:** Mastering leadership requires an iron [will](../pages/MUME_IV_as_described_in_Estonia.md#scoring-and-attributes).

## Affinity

[Sindar](./Elf.md) Elves have a natural affinity for leadership, though this does not affect the speed at which they practice the skill.
