**Old Fornost** is the western, ruined part of
[Fornost](./Fornost.md). The [Black Dagger
Guild](./Black_Dagger_Guild.md) is hidden in the cellar of a
ruined building in the northern part of this zone, and various criminals
lurk about and prey upon unsuspecting travellers. The ruins to the south
are inhabited by all kinds of malignant undead.

### Mobiles in the area

- [bandit](./bandit.md)
- [mean brigand](./mean_brigand.md)
- [thief](thief_(mobile) "wikilink")
- [dark wraith](./dark_wraith.md)
- [spirit-soldier](./spirit-soldier.md)
- [spirit](./spirit.md)
- [shade](./shade.md)
- [shadow](./shadow.md)
- [haunt](./haunt.md)

## The Castle

The ruins of the castle lie at the southern end of the old King's Road.
In addition to the [dark wraiths](./dark_wraith.md) and
[spirit-soldiers](./spirit-soldier.md) found elsewhere in the
zone, the castle is also home to some [glowing
spirits](./glowing_spirit.md). [Huge, poisonous
spiders](./Huge,_poisonous_spider.md) and
[tarantulas](./huge_tarantula.md) lurk in one of the upstairs
hallways. While most of the mobs in the area are stationary, be aware
that some [ghosts](./ghost.md) and [skeletal
warriors](./skeletal_warrior.md) roam the halls. The
[Kraken](./Super_Mobile#The_Kraken.md) makes its lair in the
flooded cellar.
