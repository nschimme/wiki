**Containers** allow you to organize your items. Some containers can be
worn on your belt, or some on your back.

Syntax for using them:

` put `&lt;item&gt;` into `&lt;container&gt;
` get `&lt;item&gt;` from `&lt;container&gt;

or for short

` put `&lt;item&gt;` `&lt;container&gt;
` get `&lt;item&gt;` `&lt;container&gt;

## Generic containers

- [bag](./bag.md) - weight 1 pound, capacity 70 pounds
- [backpack](./backpack.md) - weight 3 pounds, capacity 70 pounds
- [belt pouch](./belt_pouch.md) - weight 1 pound, capacity 25
  pounds
- [large sack](./large_sack.md) - weight 1 pound, capacity 50
  pounds
- [leather backpack](./leather_backpack.md) - weight 4 pounds,
  capacity 100 pounds
- [leather satchel](./leather_satchel.md) - weight 2 pounds,
  capacity 40 pounds
- [sable pouch](./sable_pouch.md) - weight 25 pounds, capacity 62
  pounds
