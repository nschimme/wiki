**Fill** is used to fill your lantern with [oil](./Oil.md) from a
[dark coloured flask](./Dark_coloured_flask.md) or other source.

NOTE: If you want to fill a [water skin](./Water_skin.md), use the
'[pour](./Pour.md)' command.

Example:

`> fill lantern`
