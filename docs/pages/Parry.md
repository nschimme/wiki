**Parry** is a [warrior](./Warrior.md) [skills](./Skill.md)
that will improve parrying bonus with given equipment. After a certain
increase in your skill percentage, your basic [parry
bonus](./Parry_bonus.md) will increase a given amount.

[Parry bonus](./Parry_bonus.md) (PB) will be divided among
attackers.

See also: [Dodge](./Dodge.md), [Parry
bonus](./Parry_bonus.md)
