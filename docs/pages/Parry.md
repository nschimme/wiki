# Parry

**Parry** is a [warrior](./Warrior.md) [skill](./Skill.md) focused on defense. Knowing how to parry blows allows you to live longer by giving you more time to hit and less time to bleed.

## Mechanics

- **Prerequisites:** Great [agility](../pages/MUME_IV_as_described_in_Estonia.md#scoring-and-attributes), good eyesight ([perception](../pages/MUME_IV_as_described_in_Estonia.md#scoring-and-attributes)), and lots of [muscle](../pages/MUME_IV_as_described_in_Estonia.md#scoring-and-attributes) are important.
- **Parrying Bonus (PB):** Learning this skill improves your PB with your current equipment. This bonus is divided among all attackers.
- **Scaling:** After a certain increase in your skill percentage, your basic PB will increase by a set amount.

## Affinity

[Silvan](./Elf.md) Elves are able to parry enemy blows slightly better than other races when wielding [ranged weapons](./Missile_weapon.md).
