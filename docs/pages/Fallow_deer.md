**Fallow deer** are passive level ~1-2 mobiles,
found in various locations around [Arda](./Arda.md). Their corpses
can be butchered to produce a [fine pelt](./Fine_pelt.md) and some
[meat](./Meat.md).

`A fallow deer is grazing peacefully here.`
`>`**`examine deer`**
`This small brown and white deer nibbles at the vegetation, glancing up`
`every few seconds to look for danger with its liquid brown eyes.`
