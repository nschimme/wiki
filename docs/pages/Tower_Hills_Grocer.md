A [grocer](./grocer.md) tends to the **Traveller's Shop** in
[Black Hill](./Black_Hill.md). The store stocks a variety of
clothing, tools, [containers](./container.md),
[food](./food.md) and other [items](./item.md). It is open
from 5am - 12pm and 1pm - 12am.
