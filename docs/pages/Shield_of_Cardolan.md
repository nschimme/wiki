The **shield of Cardolan** is a lightweight [shield](./Shield.md).
It's generally a less desirable option than the [shield of
Arthedain](./Shield_of_Arthedain.md), as it gives slightly less
[parry bonus](./Parry_bonus.md) for the same weight, although it
is somewhat cheaper. It loads on [Malardil](./Malardil.md), and
can also be bought from some armourers for around 7
[gold](./Gold.md) and 16 silver.

`A shield bearing the markings of Cardolan lies here.`
`>`**`examine shield`**
`This is a light metal shield bearing the markings of Cardolan.`

`>`**`consider cardolan`**
`It is a shield of Cardolan.`
`It weighs one stone and two pounds.`
