The **witch** is an aggressive level ~17-18 [mobile](./Mobile.md)
located in a tower just southeast of [Fornost](./Fornost.md). She
can cast [Burning Hands](./Burning_Hands.md) and [Lightning
Bolt](./Lightning_Bolt.md). She [tracks](./Track.md) within
her tower, but will not leave it. She loads 10 silver, [black
robe](./Black_robe.md), [heavy shoes](./Heavy_shoes.md), a
[ring](./Rings.md) and rarely the [runic
book](./Runic_book.md) with [Seeing](./Herblore#Seeing.md)
herblore.

`A wicked black witch glares at you with mad eyes.`
`> `**`examine witch`**
`>She meets your eyes with an ice-cold gaze. Despite her evil cunning and`
`power she has not yet learnt about bathing.`
